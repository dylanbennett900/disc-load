Privacy Policy

Effective 20 April 2026

This Policy explains how Dylan Bennett Sport Performance (DBSP) collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal information when you use Frisbee Track and related services. We take your privacy seriously and aim to handle your information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Who we are

Dylan Bennett, trading as Dylan Bennett Sport Performance, based in Sydney, Australia. Contact: dbsportperformance@gmail.com.

We do not deliberately collect "sensitive information" as defined under the Privacy Act (e.g. detailed health records, biometric identifiers). Training load data is performance data and is not collected for health purposes.

3. How we use your information

  • To run the app: storing sessions, calculating loads, generating prescriptions
  • To bill you: managing your subscription through Stripe
  • To communicate: sending service emails (welcome, receipts, important updates) — and, only with your opt-in consent, marketing emails you can unsubscribe from at any time
  • To improve the app: aggregate, de-identified analysis of usage patterns
  • To comply with the law: where required to do so

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your training data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Who we share it with

We use third-party services ("processors") to run the app. These services act on our instructions and are bound by their own privacy commitments:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, edge functions) — stores your account and training data. Supabase privacy
  • Google (OAuth sign-in) — for authenticating you. Google privacy
  • Google (Gemini AI) — the prescription text you request is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. We do not send your name, email, or Supabase user ID — only the training-load context needed for the prescription. Gemini API inputs are not used to train Google's models per their API terms.
  • Stripe (payments) — billing information and subscription management. Stripe privacy
  • Resend (transactional email) — sending welcome emails, receipts, and service notices. Resend privacy
  • GitHub (hosting) — serves the app static files. Access logs are kept briefly.

5. Where your data is stored

Our Supabase project is hosted in the Sydney region. Some processors (Stripe, Google, Resend) may process data in the United States or other jurisdictions. By using the service you consent to your information being processed in those countries under appropriate contractual protections.

6. How long we keep it

  • Account and training data: while your account is active, and for 30 days after you delete your account (to allow recovery of accidental deletion)
  • Billing records: 7 years from the end of the financial year, as required by Australian tax law
  • Email logs and system logs: typically 30–90 days

7. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct anything that is inaccurate or out of date
  • Delete your account and associated data (email us to request this)
  • Export your training data (email us and we will provide it as JSON)
  • Opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link in each email
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on your consent

To exercise any of these rights, email dbsportperformance@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information: HTTPS everywhere, database-level row-level security so users can only access their own data, secure password-less authentication through Google, and payment details handled only by Stripe. No system is perfectly secure. If a data breach occurs that is likely to cause serious harm, we will notify affected users and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner as soon as practicable, in line with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

9. Children

The service is not directed to children under 16. If you are under 18 you must have a parent or guardian's consent to use the service. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 16 without appropriate consent, we will delete it.

10. Cookies and tracking

The app uses essential cookies and browser storage to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. If we add analytics in future, we will update this Policy and notify you.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or an in-app notice. The "Effective" date at the top reflects the most recent update.

12. Complaints

If you have a privacy concern, email us first at dbsportperformance@gmail.com and we will do our best to resolve it. If you are not satisfied with our response, you can lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC): oaic.gov.au.
